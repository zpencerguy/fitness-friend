## Missing Features Compared With BellForge

### Account and Subscription

BellForge currently appears local-first and anonymous.

Missing:

- Login/account identity.
- User profile.
- Subscription tier state.
- Pro/free gating.
- Settings screen.
- User ID/support identity.
- Cross-device sync.

### Exercise Library

BellForge has moves embedded in workout templates, but not a full exercise library.

Missing:

- Dedicated exercise database page.
- Search across exercises.
- Filters for equipment, muscle group, difficulty, movement pattern, and level.
- Exercise detail pages.
- Real demonstration imagery or video.
- Exercise difficulty labels.
- Exercise substitutions.
- Favorite/saved exercises.

### Programs

BellForge has a weekly planner and workout templates, but not full long-form programs.

Missing:

- Multi-week programs.
- Program detail page.
- Week tabs.
- Day-by-day progression.
- Locked/unlocked workout states.
- Program start/enrollment flow.
- Program progress percentage.
- Program metadata like duration, weekly frequency, level, and goal.

### Workout Discovery and Organization

BellForge has a Workouts tab with templates and builder.

Missing:

- Clear split between official workouts, my workouts, shared workouts, and AI-generated workouts.
- Workout hub landing page.
- Shared/community workout library.
- Workout type taxonomy beyond EMOM.
- Workout search and filter.
- Featured/new workout treatment.

### Stats and Analytics

BellForge has progress heatmap, recent logs, muscle balance, and history.

Missing:

- Dedicated stats dashboard page.
- Time range filters.
- Workout type breakdown.
- Workout time totals.
- Workout days totals.
- History drill-down.
- Workout parts vs workout time toggle.
- Clear progress percentages by selected period.

### Community and Content

BellForge has no content/community surfaces yet.

Missing:

- Blog posts.
- Terminology/glossary.
- Community page.
- Shared workouts by users.
- Creator/influencer content layer.
- Partner/affiliate hub.

### AI Features

BellForge has manual builder and predefined workouts.

Missing:

- AI workout generator.
- Equipment-aware AI prompt flow.
- Goal-aware weekly workout generation.
- AI substitutions.
- AI coach recommendations after logged workouts.
- AI program generation.

### Monetization

BellForge has no monetization flow in-app.

Missing:

- Pro plan.
- Paywall screen.
- Upgrade CTA.
- Free/pro feature gating.
- Promotional offers.
- Subscription pricing.
- Lifetime or annual purchase flow.

### Mobile App UX Patterns

BellForge is web-first.

Missing:

- Native bottom navigation.
- Native-feeling full-screen routes.
- Sticky mobile CTAs.
- Full-screen modals/paywalls.
- Media-heavy exercise cards.
- Profile/settings account flow.

## Priority Opportunities For BellForge

### High Priority

1. Dedicated exercise library with search, filters, muscle tags, equipment, difficulty, and movement detail pages.
2. Program system: multi-week plans with week/day schedule, progress, and start/enroll flow.
3. Stats page: time ranges, workout days, workout minutes, workout type breakdown, and history.
4. Workout hub split: official BellForge workouts, my workouts, shared workouts, and generated workouts.
5. Account/auth and cloud sync foundation.

### Medium Priority

1. AI workout generation based on selected equipment, goal, available time, and recent muscle balance.
2. Exercise media upgrades: generated illustrations first, video later.
3. Glossary/terminology for movement patterns and training concepts.
4. Shared workout publishing.
5. Settings/profile page.

### Monetization Priority

1. Free EMOM timer as the acquisition hook.
2. Pro-gated programs, AI generation, analytics, and cloud sync.
3. Creator/influencer workout packs.
4. Annual/lifetime offer screen once there is enough premium value.

## Strategic Takeaway

KBMH is broader than a timer: it is an account-based fitness content platform with exercises, programs, stats, community, and monetization.

BellForge's strongest current differentiation is the focused EMOM timer plus equipment-aware home-gym positioning. The most important next step is not to copy every surface, but to turn BellForge from a timer plus templates into a structured training system: exercise library, multi-week programs, progress analytics, and eventually AI coaching.
